The Final Project: De-Mystifying ML

Decision Making in the Presence of Uncertainty: Differentiating Statistics and Machine Learning Models

Project Overview

Our goal is to showcase the value of what we know, by demonstrating an interactive infographic which can be used to differentiate many of the statistics and machine learning models we learned about in Bootcamp; showing when and how you could use each model with a variety of datasets.

Our Inspiration

As cohorts of the WUSTL Bootcamp, we oftentimes felt overwhelmed by the number of possible models one could use when beginning the analysis process. We decided to develop a tool that would make the decision-making process less intimidating while simultaneously providing an educational tutorial. Our interactive infographic was designed with the primary purpose of delivering the available options in a simplified, yet concise, manner. We envision it serving as a guide to help you make important decisions with your data, such as when and how to use some of the popular models. We hope you find it useful!
